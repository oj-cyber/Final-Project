Frequently Asked Questions

Find quick answers to common questions about Space Rocket Music

Account & Profile

How do I create a Space Rocket Music account?

Creating an account is easy! Follow these steps:

  1. Click the "Sign Up" button in the top right corner
  2. Enter your email address and create a password
  3. Choose a username
  4. Verify your email address by clicking the link we send you
  5. Complete your profile with your music preferences

Once completed, you'll have full access to Space Rocket Music!

I forgot my password. How can I reset it?

To reset your password:

  1. Go to the login page and click "Forgot Password"
  2. Enter the email address associated with your account
  3. Check your email for a password reset link
  4. Click the link and create a new password

If you don't receive the email within a few minutes, check your spam folder or contact our support team.

Can I change my username?

Yes, you can change your username once every 30 days. To change it:

  1. Go to your Account Settings
  2. Click on "Profile"
  3. Edit your username in the provided field
  4. Save your changes

Please note that your new username must be unique and not already in use by another member.

How do I delete my account?

We're sorry to see you go! To delete your account:

  1. Go to your Account Settings
  2. Scroll down to the "Account Management" section
  3. Click "Delete Account"
  4. Confirm your decision by entering your password

Important: Account deletion is permanent and cannot be undone. All your data, playlists, and preferences will be permanently removed.

Subscription & Premium

What are the benefits of a Premium subscription?

With a Space Rocket Music Premium subscription, you get:

  • Ad-free listening experience
  • Unlimited skips
  • Offline listening - download music to your device
  • High-quality audio (up to 320 kbps)
  • Exclusive content and early access to new releases
  • Unlimited access to our entire music library
  • Ability to create and share custom playlists

How do I cancel my subscription?

You can cancel your Premium subscription at any time:

  1. Go to your Account page
  2. Click on "Subscription"
  3. Select "Cancel Subscription"
  4. Follow the prompts to confirm cancellation

Your Premium features will remain active until the end of your current billing period. After cancellation, your account will revert to our free tier.

Can I switch between subscription plans?

Yes, you can upgrade or downgrade your subscription plan at any time:

  1. Go to your Account Settings
  2. Select "Subscription"
  3. Choose "Change Plan"
  4. Select your desired plan and confirm

If you upgrade, the new features will be available immediately. If you downgrade, the changes will take effect at the start of your next billing cycle.

Music & Playback

How do I download music for offline listening?

To download music for offline listening (Premium feature only):

  1. Find the album, playlist, or song you want to download
  2. Click the download icon (downward arrow)
  3. The content will be saved to your device
  4. Access your downloaded content in the "Downloads" section

Make sure you have enough storage space on your device. Downloaded content is only available within the Space Rocket Music app.

Why is some music not available in my region?

Music availability varies by region due to licensing restrictions set by music rights holders. While we strive to make our entire catalog available everywhere, some artists or labels restrict where their music can be streamed.

If you believe specific content should be available in your region but isn't, please contact our support team with the details, and we'll look into it.

How do I create and share playlists?

Creating and sharing playlists is easy:

To create a playlist:

  1. Go to "Your Library"
  2. Click "Create Playlist"
  3. Give your playlist a name and description
  4. Start adding songs by clicking the "+" button next to any track

To share a playlist:

  1. Open the playlist you want to share
  2. Click the three dots menu
  3. Select "Share"
  4. Choose how you want to share (link, social media, or directly with other Space Rocket Music users)

Technical Issues

The music keeps buffering. What can I do?

Buffering issues are usually related to your internet connection. Try these solutions:

  • Check your internet speed: Make sure you have a stable connection
  • Reduce audio quality: Go to Settings > Audio Quality and select a lower setting
  • Close other apps: Other applications might be using your bandwidth
  • Restart your device and router: This can resolve temporary connection issues
  • Clear cache: Go to Settings > Storage and clear the app cache
  • Update the app: Make sure you're using the latest version

If problems persist, contact our support team for further assistance.

The app crashes frequently. How can I fix this?

If the app is crashing, try these troubleshooting steps:

  1. Update the app: Make sure you have the latest version installed
  2. Restart your device: This can resolve many temporary issues
  3. Clear app cache and data: Go to your device settings, find Space Rocket Music in the app list, and clear cache/data
  4. Reinstall the app: Uninstall and then reinstall the app from the official app store
  5. Check device compatibility: Make sure your device meets the minimum requirements

If the problem continues, please contact our support team with details about your device and when the crashes occur.

Billing & Payments

How do I update my payment method?

To update your payment method:

  1. Go to your Account Settings
  2. Select "Billing" or "Payment Methods"
  3. Click "Update Payment Method"
  4. Enter your new payment details
  5. Save the changes

Your new payment method will be used for all future billing cycles.

Why was my payment declined?

Payment declines can happen for several reasons:

  • Insufficient funds in your account
  • Expired credit/debit card
  • Incorrect billing information
  • Bank or credit card company flagged the transaction as suspicious
  • International transaction restrictions

To resolve this, check your payment method details and contact your bank if necessary. You can also try using a different payment method.

How do I get a receipt for my payment?

You can access your payment receipts at any time:

  1. Go to your Account Settings
  2. Select "Billing History"
  3. Find the payment you need a receipt for
  4. Click "View Receipt" or "Download PDF"

Receipts are also automatically emailed to the address associated with your account after each successful payment.
