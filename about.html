Our Story

From a small collective to a leader in space-inspired music

Journey Through Sound

Founded in 2018, Space Rocket Music began with a vision to create a platform for artists exploring cosmic and futuristic sounds. Our collective of musicians, producers, and sound designers shared a common passion for pushing the boundaries of auditory experience.

Through years of curation and collaboration, we've launched over 50 artists and released hundreds of tracks that transport listeners to other worlds. Our innovative approach to music distribution and artist development has positioned us as a leader in the space music genre.

Today, we continue to expand our sonic universe with new artists, immersive audio experiences, and groundbreaking music technology.

Our Mission

Elevating music to new dimensions

Expanding Musical Frontiers

Our mission is to push the boundaries of music creation and consumption through innovative sound design, cutting-edge technology, and support for visionary artists exploring cosmic themes.

We believe that music has the power to transport listeners to new dimensions, evoke emotions beyond everyday experience, and connect us to the vastness of the universe.

Our current focus is on developing immersive audio experiences, supporting emerging space music artists, and creating platforms that make experimental music accessible to everyone.
